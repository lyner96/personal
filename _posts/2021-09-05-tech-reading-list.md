> Last update on 05 Sep 2021 

Here's a reading list that I think is beneficial for anyone interested in computer science, specifically artificial intelligence and data science. The edition info is stated after the author list.

<details>

<p>

    David Spiegelhalter (2020 by Pelican)<br>
</p>
</details>
